NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Swot Network Company ("SNC"), a digital marketing agency registered in Brunei Darussalam, and the undersigned client ("Receiving Party").

1. Purpose
SNC and the Receiving Party wish to explore a potential business relationship in which SNC may share information about its services, methodologies, and strategies. Both parties agree that all such information is confidential and must be protected.

2. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" includes any information disclosed by either party that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood as confidential given the nature of the information, including but not limited to:

  1. Business plans, marketing strategies, and campaign data
  2. Client lists, pricing structures, and financial information
  3. Proprietary tools, templates, reports, and processes
  4. Any communication between SNC and the Receiving Party

3. Obligations
The Receiving Party agrees to: (a) keep Confidential Information strictly confidential; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from SNC; (c) use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating the potential business relationship.

4. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known by the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

5. Duration
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of two (2) years from the date of signing, or until such time as the Confidential Information enters the public domain through no fault of the Receiving Party.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Brunei Darussalam. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Bandar Seri Begawan.

7. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and agreements.

By signing below, the Receiving Party acknowledges reading, understanding, and agreeing to all terms of this Agreement.
